Twist

     Twist, developed by Doist, is a team communication and collaboration platform designed to promote calm, focused work over the constant interruptions of real-time chat tools. Unlike traditional messaging apps, Twist emphasizes asynchronous communication, helping teams reduce distractions, manage time zone differences, and respond more thoughtfully. This makes it especially useful for remote and distributed teams seeking to maintain productivity without the stress of instant replies. The platform is organized around channels and threads, allowing conversations to stay clear, structured, and easy to follow. Each thread focuses on a specific topic, much like an email chain, but with a more collaborative, modern feel. This threaded structure keeps information organized, prevents important messages from getting lost in a flood of chat, and makes it easy to revisit past discussions.

     Twist also includes features like direct messaging, mentions, file sharing, and integrations with popular tools such as GitHub, Trello, and Google Drive. Its clean design and minimal notifications support deep work and reduce digital noise. Available on desktop, web, and mobile, Twist syncs seamlessly across devices. For teams seeking a long-term, sustainable communication tool that values clarity and focus over speed, Twist offers a refreshing alternative to traditional team chat apps.
