Focus@Will

      Focus@will is a subscription service that offers sequenced playlists of instrumental music. All music available at Focus@will is separated into categories including classical, cinematic, ambient etc. The app offers a personalized feed and also includes a timer function and productivity tracker. The tool was founded in 2012 by a musician and tech entrepreneur Will Henshall, Focus@will was born from a desire to merge music and neuroscience to enhance productivity. Henshall, known for his work with his type of music, collaborated with co-founders John Vitale and Graham Lyus to develop a platform that uses specially curated instrumental music to help users maintain focus and reduce distractions.

      The service offers a variety of music channels, such as classical, up-tempo, focus spa, cinematic, and ambient, each designed to cater to different concentration needs. By analyzing user interactions, the app personalizes the listening experience, adapting to individual preferences and work habits. In addition to it's music offerings, Focus@will includes features like a session timer and a productivity tracker, enabling users to monitor their focus levels over time. These tools aim to provide a comprehensive solution for individuals seeking to improve their concentration during tasks such as studying, writing, and/or working. By integrating music with productivity, Focus@will offers a unique way to enhancing focus and efficiency in various environments

User Interface

Focus@Will Dashboard

      After logging in to your account, you will see at the top center, there are three circular control buttons. The left button sets a session timer, helping to manage the focused work intervals. The middle button is the play and pause control, while the right button skips to the next track. On the top right, there’s a dropdown button showing the current music channel, allowing users to switch between different audio genres. Below the green button, is the user’s email that is being displayed, indicating the active session. The background image also changes based on the theme, and the music, adding a calming visual experience to match the audio environment of the music. Users can adjust the volume or enable productivity mode for tailored music scientifically designed to support focus. The overall layout is minimalist, ensuring that nothing distracts from the primary goal—deep concentration.
