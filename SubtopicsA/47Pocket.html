Pocket

     Pocket is your personal “save-for-later” app, thoughtfully designed to help you discover, save, and return to the most interesting, inspiring, and insightful stories from across the web—whenever it suits you. Whether you're catching up on the latest think pieces, bookmarking an investigative feature for later, or saving a tutorial for future reference, Pocket allows you to create your own personalized library of high-quality content.

     With a single click, you can save articles, videos, and webpages from your browser, email, or apps like Twitter and Flipboard, seamlessly syncing them across your devices so they're always available—online or offline. Pocket transforms scattered reading into an organized and intentional experience, making it easy to revisit what matters most to you, even when you’re on the go or away from Wi-Fi. Each week, Pocket curates a list of its Top-Saved Stories, giving you a snapshot of what the global Pocket community found most thought-provoking and worth sharing. These selections range from timely opinion pieces and cultural critiques to motivational essays and in-depth explorations—like the paradox of the screen-time report, the growing popularity of the “let them” theory, or a surprising investigative look into the life and legacy of video game icon Luigi. These stories not only reflect what people are reading but also what’s resonating most in today’s information-rich landscape.This curated experience helps users stay informed and inspired without the overwhelm of endless scrolling.

User Interface

Pocket Dashboard

     Pocket’s user interface is designed for ease of use, helping users quickly discover, save, and organize content from across the web into a personalized, accessible library. Upon opening the app, users are greeted with a clean, minimalist layout where saved articles are neatly organized in a simple, scrollable list. The home screen features a curated selection of Pocket’s Top-Saved Stories, showcasing the most popular or thought-provoking content that others are reading, making it easy to discover new topics or dive into the latest trends. Each saved article is displayed with a title, snippet, and relevant tags, offering quick insights into its content. Users can customize their reading experience with adjustable text size, font style, and a distraction-free reading mode that strips away clutter for a more focused view. Dark mode and sepia themes are also available, enhancing readability in different lighting environments. Additionally, Pocket’s built-in text-to-speech feature allows users to listen to saved articles on the go, transforming reading time into an audio experience.
