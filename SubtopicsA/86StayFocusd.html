StayFocusd

      Stay Focusd is the ultimate productivity booster for anyone who struggles with online distractions. It is a free Chrome extensions that can help you to take control of your own time by limiting the amount of time that can spend on time-wasting websites. Whether it’s social media, video streaming ir endless new scrolling, StayFocusd ensures the your focus stays where it should be and on your work. Once you set a daily time limit for a group of distracting sites, StayFocusd begins tracking, When your allotted time runs out, the sites are blocked for the rest of the day. No cheating, no loopholes. You can also customize which sites to block, how long you’re allowed on them, and even which days and hours the restrictions apply.

      Features like “Nuclear Option” can block your access entirely for a set of time with no way to turn it off. The “Allowed Sites” list lets you to whitelist your selected helpful sites so that you’re not cut off from what you need. StayFocusd also offers extra features like “Require Challenge”, where you must complete a tough tasks before making changes. And in the feature “In App Blocking”, you can cover new tabs or popups. Weather you’re a student, a worker, or just someone trying to break bad habits, StayFocusd gives you the structure to stay focus and productive at the same time. StayFocusd also support you to help you stay disciplined and productive without constantly battling temptation thus, one focused session at a time.

User Interface

StayFocusd Dashboard

      The StayFocusd dashboard displays the Blocked side section, where users can limit time on distracting websites. A toggle at the top left indicates the extension is active. The central panel also shows the Default group, with a daily max usage time set to 1 hour, and a time left indicator displaying how much remains. Users can Add Blocked Websites to include new sites in the group.

      The left sidebar offers more controls, Active days and Active hours let users set when restrictions apply, while allowed sites list exceptions. Features like The Nuclear Option enforce extreme blocking, and require Challenge adds difficulty to changing settings. Users can also customize, import and export settings, or pair it with another tool like StayFree, a tool for managing digital habits.
