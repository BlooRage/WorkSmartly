Zapier

      Have you ever considered making your apps co-function well without your doing manual effort to accomplish it? Indeed zapier comes in. It is a very powerful automation platform that connects your favorite apps like Gmail, Google Sheets, Trello, Slack, and many others so that they work together without your intervention. In a manner you could think of it as an under the scenes assistant that relieves your mind most often from repetitive tasks while you focus on what really matters. With zapier you create automated workflows called Zaps that will trigger specific actions for events in other apps. To give you examples: Automatically send the answers to a Google Form into a Google Sheet, or notify the whole of your team in Slack whenever a new task is created in Trello.

      It runs smoothly in the background, eliminating the need to do repetitive tasks again and again, once configured. It would best suit a person who uses multiple tools every single day to try and optimize as much as possible on activities at the office, be it managing a business, organizing a team, or trying to stay on top of your tasks. No, you do not need to learn to code. Just point click and let zapier do the work. Start your exploration today and see how much more efficient your everyday workflow can become with automation because it is right at your fingertips. Connect apps like Gmail, Slack, Trello and many more to automate routines with ease. Save hours every week by letting zapier handle the little things so you can focus on the big picture.

User Interface

Zapier Dashboard

      Zapier's interface is clean and simple, intended to assist users in creating and organizing automated workflows termed as Zaps easily. The left side panel is where all the main sections are presented Dashboard, Zaps, Transfers, Tables, and Apps. The center panel leads users through setting up automation through a step by step editor. Every step provides easy to understand instructions to select a trigger app, event, and action to follow.

      The interface is created with simplicity in mind through liberal use of white space, clean icons, and collapsible panels. Feedback and success or failure indicators build user confidence in real time. The top bar includes search, help, and account settings. Zap templates are recommended to accelerate automation setup. The ui overall balances power and simplicity for users of all technical skill levels.
