Cold Turkey

      Cold Turkey is a free website blocker tailored for studying or focusing on work, Cold Turkey Blocker makes it impossible to stop the block because they use a robust locking system. The tool also allows you to block anything from specific websites and applications, with a choice to schedule it or not. The story of Cold Turkey began in 2010 when Felix Belzile, created a simple program to help a friend in blocking access to a game called World of Warcraft for better studying habits. Recognizing the broader need for such a tool, Felix developed Cold Turkey into a comprehensive application designed to help users overcome digital distractions. Cold Turkey's standout feature is its unyielding commitment to enforcing focus. Once a block is initiated, it's virtually impossible to bypass, even for tech-savvy users. The application employs measures like preventing uninstallation during active blocks and resisting system clock changes to ensure stay on track.

      Beyond website blocking, Cold turkey offers tools like writer, which transforms your computer into a distraction-free writing environment, and Micromanager, allowing only selected applications to run during focus sessions. These features cater to a wide range of users, from students to professionals seeking to enhance productivity. By choosing Cold Turkey, users will commit to a disciplined approach to managing their digital environment, fostering better habits, and achieving their goals without the interference of online distraction. Additionally, its customizable scheduling and break timers help users stay focused while maintaining balance.

User Interface

Cold Turkey Dashboard

      In the Cold Turkey Dashboard, specifically under the “Block” tab within “Website & App Blocks.” A block named “Distraction” can be turn off and on, toggling this button and setting it to green means that it’s “On” while the opposite is “Off”. it’s configured to block distractions at all times, with no scheduled breaks and applies to all users. When the block is configured to “Unlocked”, the changes can still be made. The green plus with the word New Block button allows users to create new block lists, while the green plus icon in the top right corner also provides options to add blocks. The Three-line icon beside it opens more settings for block management. The left sidebar offers navigation to Dashboard, Blocks, Statistics, and Settings. Then At the bottom, you’ll find the view lock settings, and status if there is an extension or not.
