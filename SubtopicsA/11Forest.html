Forest

      Forest is an innovative productivity application that transforms time management through a creative focus system. Developed by Seekrtech and released in 2016, the app was designed to help individuals avoid distractions and stay focused in a world filled with constant digital interruptions. Its unique concept encourages users to plant a virtual tree that grows while they remain focused on their task. If they leave the app to check social media or use other unrelated applications, the tree begins to wither, offering a visual reminder of the cost of losing focus. This simple yet effective system helps users build discipline, stay mindful of their time, and improve their attention span through an interactive and meaningful method.

      What makes Forest even more impactful is its connection to environmental efforts. As users accumulate focused time, they earn virtual coins that can be spent to plant real trees through Forest’s partnership with global tree planting organizations. The app features a clean and calming interface with options to customize the experience, such as choosing different types of trees and adding ambient background sounds to enhance concentration. Forest is especially popular among students, remote workers, and professionals who seek both improved productivity and a way to make a positive difference. It stands out as a tool that combines personal growth with environmental responsibility in an engaging and thoughtful way.

User Interface

FOREST - MOBILE DASHBOARD

      The Forest mobile dashboard presents a highly interactive and visually appealing interface designed to promote focus through a gamified approach. On the main screen, users are greeted with a timer and a message indicating recent accomplishments, such as staying focused for 70 minutes. A “Plant” button allows users to begin a new focus session, during which a virtual tree grows as long as they remain on task. If they exit the app, the tree dies, visually reinforcing the value of staying present. Swiping through the app reveals an “Overview” section where users can view their growing forest, with each tree representing a completed session. A weekly bar graph below visualizes time spent focusing each day. The “Forest Ranking” section adds a competitive element by displaying how users compare to friends in terms of focus time. Finally, the “Treehouse” section introduces rewards, allowing users to redeem earned coins for special upgrades. Navigation is smooth and intuitive, with simple icons and gestures guiding users through tracking, rewards, and statistics.
