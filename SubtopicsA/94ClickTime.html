ClickTime

      ClickTime is a powerful time tracking software that can monitor regular hours along with overtime. ClickTime allows custom projects, set billing rates, add tasks, allocate hours to each task and assign them accordingly. The tool was established in 1997, ClickTime was among the early pioneers in the software as a Service (SaaS) industry. It’s flagship product, ClickTime Web Timesheet, was developed to meet the needs of professional services firms, offering one of the first solutions for offline time tracking with web-based reporting. Now, ClickTime has evolved into a comprehensive platform for time and resource management.

      The tool’s robust features set includes detailed insights into project costs, profitability, and staff utilization. Users can track employee hours, meet project dealing, analyze budget targets, and ensure seamless compliance. The platform supports real-time data access, allowing for swift decision-making and resource reallocation when plans change. Additionally, Clicktime offers tools for expense tracking, time off management, and DCAA (Defense Contract of Defense – responsible for auditing Department of Defense contracts in the United Stats) compliance, making it suitable for a wide range of industries and organizational needs. By choosing ClickTime, organizations can enhance workforce efficiency, improve financial performance, and deliver excellent client service through accurate time tracking and resource management. Its intuitive interface and customizable reports empower teams to stay informed and agile, adapting quickly to evolving project demands.

User Interface

ClickTime Dashboard

      In the ClickTime dashboard in Week View, users can use this for tracking work hours. The red box highlights the time entry grid, where users log hours per project and task. Each row lists the client, project, and task type, followed by boxes to input hours for each day. The trash icon deletes entries, while the plus icon adds a time entry button adds new rows. The top bar helps to switch views, and the save and submit for Approval button let users finalize their logs and progress bars under each day give a quick visual of logged hours. Users can also add notes to each time entry for context or reporting. The dashboard’s layout makes it easy to spot missing entries and stay compliant with time-tracking requirements. Time off, break periods, and billable vs. non-billable hours can also be tracked. Custom reports can be generated to analyze productivity, client hours, or budget adherence.
