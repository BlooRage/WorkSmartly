Momentum

     Momentum is a beautifully designed browser extension that transforms your new tab page into an inspiring and calming workspace, designed to help you stay focused, energized, and mindful throughout your day. Upon opening a new tab, Momentum greets you with a serene background image, often featuring stunning landscapes or minimalist artwork, paired with an inspirational quote to set a positive tone. The interface is clean and clutter-free, with essential tools like a customizable to-do list, weather updates, and a focus timer prominently displayed, allowing you to easily track your tasks and stay organized.

     Momentum’s minimalist design encourages mindfulness and focus, removing distractions while subtly motivating you to stay on top of your goals. You can personalize your experience by setting daily goals, adding personal notes, and choosing from a variety of themes, making your browser a space that aligns with your productivity style. With Momentum, your browser becomes more than just a tool; it’s a source of calm and motivation that helps you maintain focus and stay energized throughout your workday. The homepage features a daily inspirational quote that can provide an uplifting start to your day and serve as a reminder of your objectives. Momentum also includes a to-do list, allowing you to track tasks and priorities directly from your browser, ensuring you stay organized without having to switch between apps. You can integrate your calendar to get a visual snapshot of upcoming events, helping you plan your day with ease. For users who enjoy customization.

User Interface

Momentum Dashboard

     Momentum's user interface is designed to promote focus, calm, and productivity with a minimalist and aesthetically pleasing layout. Upon opening a new tab, the central focus is a high-quality background image, which changes daily to offer fresh, tranquil visuals that set a positive and calming tone. At the top of the page, a greeting message and motivational quote are displayed, helping users start their day with inspiration and mindfulness. Below the quote, there’s a space for users to set their daily goal, allowing them to stay focused on their top priority for the day. The interface also includes a simple to-do list, where tasks can be added and checked off as they’re completed, further enhancing productivity. To the right, users can find a search bar and quick links to frequently used websites, keeping everything organized and easily accessible.
