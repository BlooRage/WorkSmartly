Superhuman

     Superhuman is a premium email application that was launched in 2014 by Rahul Vohra. In the beginning, it was only available through invitations, which made many users even more curious about it. Because of its unique and fast approach to email, Superhuman quickly became popular among professionals, students, and busy individuals looking for a better way to manage their emails. One of the main reasons why people love using Superhuman is because of its incredible speed. Emails open instantly, and users can reply using smart keyboard shortcuts. This helps save time and allows people to stay focused on their tasks without delays. Instead of showing everything at once, Superhuman helps you focus by sorting your emails smartly. Its “inbox triage” feature automatically filters incoming messages, placing only the most important emails in your main inbox and moving less urgent ones to a separate folder. This helps users avoid distractions and spend more time on what truly matters. With this smart system, managing emails becomes faster and less stressful, especially for people who receive a lot of messages every day. Another helpful tool is the built-in AI writing assistant that checks grammar, improves sentence structure, and helps users write more professionally. This is useful for students writing formal emails and workers communicating with clients. Superhuman also supports teamwork by allowing users to collaborate and share emails with others. It works smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices. Superhuman is more than just an email app it’s a smart, fast, and reliable tool for staying productive, organized, and stress-free.

User Interface

Superhuman UI

      Superhuman is a modern email app designed for faster and more organized email use. Its interface is clean and simple, so users can focus better and not get distracted. The inbox only shows key info like who sent the message, the subject, and the time received, which helps keep it neat. On the left side, folders like Inbox, Sent, Drafts, and Archive are clearly arranged to make navigation easy. When you compose an email, a centered text box pops up that’s simple and smooth to use. One of the best things about Superhuman is its keyboard shortcuts that let you quickly do actions without using the mouse. It also has useful features like email tracking, reminders to follow up, and smart replies to help you respond faster. Overall, the UI of Superhuman is built for people who want speed, simplicity, and better control of their email experience.
