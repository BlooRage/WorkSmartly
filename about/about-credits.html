Meet the Team

      We are a group of students from the University of the East – Manila, under the College of Computer Studies and Systems (CCSS). This project was developed as a final requirement for our course, CIP1101 – Integrative Programming and Technologies. Through this work, we aimed to apply our skills, collaborate effectively, and deliver a system that reflects our learning and passion in the field of Information Technology.
