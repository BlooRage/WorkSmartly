Ziflow

     Ziflow is an online proofing and collaboration platform that streamlines the creative review and approval process. It allows teams to review, comment on, and approve digital content such as images, videos, PDFs, and web banners in a centralized location. Founded in 2016 by creative workflow experts, Ziflow was developed to address inefficiencies in traditional feedback processes, which often involve scattered comments and long email chains. Its interface supports real-time collaboration, version tracking, and automated workflows, making it suitable for creative, marketing, and design teams.

     Ziflow enhances productivity, reduces feedback errors, and shortens project timelines. It supports over 1,200 file types and integrates with tools like Adobe Creative Cloud, Slack, Trello, and project management platforms. Built-in automation ensures stakeholders are notified at each stage of the review process, maintaining structure and progress. The platform improves review clarity, simplifies content approvals, and increases consistency in delivering creative assets. User Interface

Ziflow Home

     The Ziflow UI offers a user-friendly experience centered on visual proof management. Its top navigation allows seamless switching between "Products" and "Campaigns," organizing projects effectively. Below, a clear filter bar enables efficient sorting of proofs by status. Each proof is visually represented by a thumbnail, providing immediate context alongside essential details like filename, version, upload time, and current status.

     Interactive elements appear upon hovering, and a single click grants access to detailed proof views. The prominent "+ New Proof" button encourages quick initiation of the review process. The hierarchical left sidebar facilitates easy navigation through workspaces, clients, products, campaigns, and folders. The interface prioritizes visual clarity and streamlined access to proofing workflows, making it intuitive for users to manage and collaborate on creative content.
