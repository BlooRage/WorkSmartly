IFTTT

      If This Then That or IFTTT is an automation platform to connect your apps, devices, and services together in the smartest of ways. You can create custom workflows using conditional statements also termed as applets that follow an easy logic If this happens then do that. In simple words, it isn't too simple. You can link up from dissimilar devices over different platforms and make sure they work automatically without any technical know how. Just want to have your smart lights off when you leave your house? Done. Want all your social media accounts to post the same content simultaneously Double done. You can even have a watch of the day sent to your phone or save the email attachments directly to your cloud account storage.

      There is no limit, whether you are looking to give some systematic shape to your daily routines or improve your workflows for business IFTTT is definitely the answer for a flexible yet robust automation of anything. It will take off your repetitive tasks, and you can do more important things with your time. The best part You don't need to know much about technical wizardry to use it. Just choose a trigger, set an action, and IFTTT will do the rest. Open your imagination and start creating your own automated experiences. You would be surprised at what you can create. With a growing number of supported apps and services, IFTTT is a powerful tool for both personal and professional productivity.

User Interface

IFTTT Dashboard

      IFTTT has an extremely clean and welcoming UI that is focused on the simplicity of automation. Its most frequently visited applet pages, explore tab, and account settings are easily accessed on the home page through the top bar. Applets automation consisting of triggers and actions are presented in clean, card like tiles with toggle switches for turning them on or off.

      Creating an applet is a straightforward, step by step process selecting a service to do This and another to do That. Icons, color, and short text make it intuitive. Accessibility to non-techies is prioritized, with personal and smart home automations in the forefront. The overall design ensures that even the most complex automations are easily understood and executed. With a user-friendly interface, anyone can dive into automation without feeling overwhelmed.
