Other Productivity Tools

     Not all productivity tools fit into one specific category, but every tool here can make a significant impact on your daily routine. This category includes a diverse range of apps and services that help with everything from managing passwords to blocking distractions and improving focus. Whether you're trying to organize your tasks better, keep your passwords safe, or minimize interruptions during work, these tools cover various needs to boost your efficiency.

     This category includes Password Managers like LastPass and 1Password to securely store and manage passwords, as well as Browser Extensions / Utilities such as Momentum and Alfred that improve your browsing and workflow experience. Deep Work & Distraction Blocking tools like Freedom and Cold Turkey help you stay focused and block distractions, while Specialized Productivity Tools such as WorkFlowy and Focuster provide customized ways to manage tasks and time. These tools are perfect for those who need specific solutions for their productivity needs.
