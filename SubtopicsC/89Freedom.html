Freedom

      Freedom is a tool that manages screen time and blocks websites. Freedom is the only app that can sync blocking sessions across all devices, it also offers comprehensive app blocking on desktops, website exceptions, and immersive focus and music creating a complete environment for distraction-free work. Behind the powerful productivity solution is Fred Stutzman, who created freedom in 2009. He was a Ph.D. student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, frustrated by his own inability to focus, he created a tool To combat his own distractions, he developed a simple internet-blocking tool and soon realized that many others shared the same need for digital discipline and It quickly evolved into a widely adopted platform.

      Today, Freedom supports millions of users worldwide, helping them build healthier digital habits. with it's ability to schedule recurring focus sessions, create customized blocklists, and activate "Locked Mode" for stronger commitment, it's especially popular among writers, students, professionals, and creatives. with it's seamless cross-platform, customizable controls, and calming background soundscapes, Freedom doesn't just block distraction, it builds a better work-life rhythm. Freedom isn't just about blocking distraction, it's about regaining control of your time and attention in an always-connected world. Choosing Freedom means choosing your clarity, being in deep focus, and a smarter way to work across every device you own.

User Interface

Freedom Dashboard

      In the Freedom dashboard, users can easily start a focus session by setting a timer and choosing whether to block websites, desktop apps, or all digital distractions. On the right, you can also specify which device the session applies to, giving you full control across multiple platforms. The left panel allows users to add new devices, enable Locked Mode for unbreakable sessions, and play calming focus sounds like “London” to enhance concentration. Below that, the Blocklists section lets you create and name custom lists—like “Socials” or “News”—and manually add sites to block during sessions. The interface is clean, intuitive, and designed for fast setup, making it ideal for studying, working, or deep focus sessions. You can also schedule recurring sessions in advance, helping you build healthy digital habits over time. With just a few clicks, Freedom makes it easy to reclaim control over your attention and time.
