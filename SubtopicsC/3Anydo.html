Any.do

      Any.do is a smart productivity application that has supported millions of users since its launch in 2011 by the company Any.do Ltd. Known for its sleek and minimalist interface, the app combines task management, calendar scheduling, and reminders into a single streamlined experience. Its signature feature, Moment, encourages users to begin each day by reviewing and prioritizing their tasks, offering a focused and intentional start to the morning. Users can easily create and organize task lists, add subtasks, attach files, and set recurring reminders based on their schedules. The app also includes voice recognition for hands-free task creation, making it especially convenient for users on the go. Its flexible design supports both simple to-do lists and more complex task structures for work, school, or personal life.

      Collaboration is another core strength of Any.do. Users can share lists, assign tasks, and receive real-time updates, making it an effective tool for coordinating group efforts at home or at work. The app syncs across multiple platforms including smartphones, tablets, desktops, and smartwatches, ensuring a seamless experience regardless of the device being used. Integration with calendar apps and smart reminders enhances daily planning and organization. With its balance of intuitive design and powerful features, Any.do functions not only as a planner but also as a personal assistant that helps users stay organized, make thoughtful decisions, and build strong time management habits.

User Interface

Any.do - My Day

      Any.do delivers a task management interface that balances simplicity with versatility. The center screen focuses on the "Today" view, where users can easily view and manage current tasks. A prominent "Add Task" button allows quick input of new tasks, streamlining task creation. The left sidebar includes views for "Tomorrow," "Upcoming," and "Someday," offering short- and long-term planning. What makes Any.do stand out is its combined task and calendar view, allowing users to manage events and to-dos in a single interface. Additional features like reminders, subtasks, recurring tasks, and voice input make it convenient and accessible. With seamless syncing across devices, Any.do caters to both simple daily lists and more complex scheduling needs, offering a flexible yet straightforward productivity solution.
